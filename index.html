A Climathon is a 24 hour hackathon to produce a solution that addresses a city's mitigation or adaption challenge. This Climathon is organized to occur with other cities across the world. For the purpose of this climathon, we are seeking to start solutions, which are profitable within 5 years or are of compelling social value to warrant social investment and action. Karlsruhe is a young city with a deep climate science and entreprenuerial culture. As well as being a signatory for the Global Covenant of Mayors, the city hosts a regional climate research center at KIT-Nord, ... [other instutitions here]. On its own, the city offers multiple programs such as... If you look beyond the local science community, you will find an entreprenuerial network that provides [services] from [entrepreurial agencies]. This climathon leverages these assets for hackathon solutions that help make them a reality. To selected projects, we help provide guidance and support to move efforts to the next level. Since this effort originates from the UNFCCC, access to these networks as well as the potential to participate in KIC-Climate Entreprenuerial Challenges is highly possible when developed solutions can benefit cities other than Karlsruhe.

Event Location: Future Room by Quartier Zukunft

Karlsruhe is unique in its capacity to address the localized effects of climate change through its efforts in Sustainable Development. Quartier Zukunft has created a space where scientist can work with local citizens to study how different theories and methods can improve the local community in Oststadt.
